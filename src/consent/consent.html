We Respect Your Privacy

To make your browsing experience safer and more supportive, we need your permission to access the text content on the webpages you visit.

Why do we need your permission?

Our extension helps protect your mental well-being by analyzing the text content of the pages you visit. This allows us to identify potentially harmful content that might affect your mental health and send you helpful notifications. We do not collect any personal data, only the text content is analyzed.

Thank you for your consent!
